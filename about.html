About Me

I graduated from Ohio University in May 2020. I studied Visual Communication: Information Design - Interactive Multimedia and got a minor in Marketing. I will soon be starting my career as a User Experience Specialist at Nationwide.

Prior to working for Nationwide, I was a Web & UX Designer for Ohio University's Division of Student Affairs Communication & Marketing team. I also interned at Viasat in 2019 as their User Experience Visual Design Intern. Before that, I worked at McGraw-Hill Education as their Marketing Design Intern. I was the Graphic Designer for Ohio University's Office of Global Opportunities from 2017 – 2018.

I was involved in many organizations at Ohio University. I was the President of the Ohio University Multimedia Society (OUMS), Vice President of Project C, and I designed for Thread Fashion Magazine. OUMS is a professional networking student organization that focuses on career readiness and providing networking opportunities to students interested in multimedia. Project C is a student organization that creates multimedia work for local nonprofits.

I strive to challenge myself to try new things and develop my skills as I grow as a designer.
