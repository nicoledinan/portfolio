About Me

I am a senior at Ohio University, studying Visual Communication: Information Design - Interactive Multimedia with a minor in Marketing. I am currently looking for full-time opportunities in Southern California related to web design, user experience (UX/UI) and/or marketing design.

I am currently a Graphic and Web/UX Designer for Ohio University's Division of Student Affairs Communication & Marketing. I recently completed an internship with Viasat as their User Experience Visual Design Intern. Previously, I worked at McGraw-Hill Education as their Marketing Design Intern. I was the Graphic Designer for Ohio University's Office of Global Opportunities from 2017 – 2018. I also work as a freelance designer for local and remote clients.

I am involved in many organizations at Ohio University. I am the President of the Ohio University Multimedia Society (OUMS), Vice President of Project C (a student organization that creates multimedia work for local nonprofits), and I have designed for Thread Fashion Magazine.

I strive to challenge myself to try new things and develop my skills as I grow as a designer.
